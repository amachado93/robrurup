    • about

    Robert Rurup is a woodworker from northern New Jersey. He began learning the craft at the age of twelve, building shelves and practical pieces of furniture with his father. His interest grew during years of high school shop class, and steered him towards woodworking on a professional level. "Making" has now become part of his daily routine.

    He experiments with both traditional and modern techniques, not limited to the medium of wood. Other undertaken art forms include pottery, printmaking, and needlework.

    The aim of his work is to cherish the element of simplicity, emphasizing the beauty of natural material while serving a fully functional purpose.
